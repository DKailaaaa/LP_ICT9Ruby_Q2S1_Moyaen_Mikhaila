Characters Background Ability
Blossom

-She is one of the main character and the protagonist of the film.

-Her official color is pink

-She is the most mature in their group since she's the leader and has a bossy attitude.

 Her ability is an ice breath, which allows her to freeze someone by blowing her breath.
Buttercup

-She is one of the main character and the protagonist of the film.

-Her official color is green

-She often fights hard and loves to get dirty since she symbolizes joy, happiness, and youth.

 Her special power or ability is that she can curl her tongue.
Bubbles

-She is one of the main character and the protagonist of the film.

-Her official color is blue

-She is the youngest in their group. She was soft hearted and pure, but she was still a crybaby.

Her power is called "Spit power" wherein she can turn into a superhuman when mad.
